About Us

At Arrakis Software Solutions, we are dedicated to helping our clients discover the most suitable and effective software solutions — carefully tailored to their unique needs and goals. We believe in true partnership, working side by side with our clients throughout the journey — collaborating, innovating, and achieving success together.

Founded in 2016 by Eran Peretz and Aviram Dayan, Arrakis brings the combined expertise of seasoned full-stack developers and technology consultants. With a proven track record in end-to-end software development, system architecture, R&D leadership, and hands-on consulting, we have successfully supported startups and organizations navigating complex digital transformations.

Today, Arrakis Software Solutions continues to provide comprehensive software development and consulting services, precisely customized to meet the evolving demands of businesses and organizations.
